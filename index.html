The Luxury Hotel

Enjoy a Luxury Experience

Hotel, inspired by Gautam Buddha’s family name, means delightful in Irish, and true to its meaning, the hotel delights its customers with the high level of service it provides them.

Luxury Hotels is a luxurious full-service hotel that perfectly represents Hyderabad’s renowned tradition of hospitality, warmth and an extravagant lifestyle with its world-class services and facilities. Situated at Raj Bhavan Road, Luxury hotel & towers beautifully blends space and functionality in a contemporary ambience.
